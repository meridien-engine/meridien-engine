# Meridien Store Guide

## Return Policy
We offer a full 30-day money back guarantee on all unopened items.
If the item is opened, it is subject to a 15% restocking fee.
Items damaged by the customer are not eligible for return.

## Store Hours
Our flagship retail location in San Francisco is open Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM Pacific Time.
We are closed on weekends and all major national holidays.

## Shipping
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. 
Expedited shipping is available for an additional $15 and takes 1-2 business days.
Free shipping is applied automatically to all orders over $100.
